ATR/Airbus: Data Analyst

My main experience was as a data analyst at ATR, I was in charge of analysis of a large dataset of aircraft maintenance reports. ATR, as they are aircraft designer and constructor, have multiple partnerships with other companies. Some of those companies are MRO (Maintenance Repair Overhall), those kind of companies have the role of doing the maintenance of aircraft.

Few things about the aircraft industry

In the aircraft industry, every modifications, every step linked to any process that concerns an aircraft is recorded and so ins the process of aircraft maintenance. In one hand, the EASA, an European authority, that is in charge of the regularization of aircraft design, construction, operation issues most of the constraints that should be applied on every type of aircraft (in Europe) . In the other hand, aircraft manufacturer such as Airbus or ATR, issues the documentation that concerns the process linked to their specific aircraft. Just to name a few of them:

  • MRBR: Maintenance Review Board Report
  • MPD: Maintenance Planning Document
  • IPC: Illustration Part Catalog

The aircraft industry has a very strict regulation, and that resulted to a very high level of safety (Air transportation being the safest way of traveling).

My work at ATR

So back to my work, I had to retrieve every kind of information that could be used from these reports. The reports themselves included: the maintenance duration, the default description, the parts changed, the tasks. So, from those information, I could get a model of maintenance cost over aircraft age, redundancy of defaults, and most costing tasks.



I2M laboratory

This project was about studying the characteristics of the interface made with metal and elastomer., working mainly with Matlab, I worked on image processing to do measurement.

During the first part of the project, we would test and measure the elastomer characteristics on a specific stand, with specific software, and taking picture at the same time. Then the second part is to measure the elongation of the elastomer sample with Matlab. The third step is to compare the software measurement with the matlab results.

For second part of the projects, we would them do the same operation but only with different kind of samples, an elastomer stuck with a piece of metal. And for the second part, we would use different samples, with different artificial aging process. That part allowed us to measure the effect of aging for the interface and let us know how does it behave with time.
